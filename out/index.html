Mission

You are supporting more than just another NFT initiative when you support ToyMories. We're in discussions with numerous partners, arranging ways to give back to our communities. This idea has the potential to reach the worlds most impoverished areas, but it must begin in our own neighborhoods.


ToyMories will hold a semi annual toy drive at the beginning of the school year as well as around Christmas time. 10% of the revenues will be used to collect and deliver toys to children in need. We'll not only provide the funding and resources, but also knowledge on the areas most in need as we encourage our holders to go out locally and make a difference.
